Provide configurations for the LDAP servers that Jenkins should search, minimum one. Several servers with different configurations can be provided and Jenkins will query them in turn in the configured order.

To try to provide some form of consistency from normal operations; if there is a communication issue with one of the servers during a login or search attempt with one of the configured servers, any following servers will not be tried, in case of the same usernames existing on the different servers then users the lowered orders servers will still be shadowed by those of higher order.
